## Design Context

### Users
**Cikgu (Teachers)**. The primary context is **mobile usage on the field** during Sports Day. The interface must be highly legible in bright sunlight, have large tap targets for quick data entry, and feel extremely responsive/real-time.

### Brand Personality
**Modern, Sports Vibe, Burning Spirit**. The interface should evoke energy, excitement, and professional efficiency. It's about "semangat membara" (burning spirit) - reflecting the heat and passion of a sports competition.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Bold & Energetic**. High contrast, vibrant accent colors (Orange/Red/Blue), strong typography (Figtree), and dynamic layouts. It should feel like a premium sports app (e.g., Nike, ESPN, Strava) rather than a boring administrative tool.

### Design Principles
1. **Mobile-First Utility**: Optimized for one-handed use on the field. Essential actions (entering scores, checking schedules) must be front and center.
2. **High-Visibility Contrast**: Clear separation of elements and high-contrast text to ensure usability in outdoor conditions.
3. **Real-Time Feedback**: Use subtle animations and "live" indicators to show that data is being synced and rankings are updating instantly.
4. **Vibrant Accents**: Use "burning" colors (Orange/Red) to signify energy and action, while keeping the background clean (Light Mode) for readability.
5. **Bold Typography**: Large, clear headings that establish a strong hierarchy and convey the "spirit" of the event.

## Dashboard Design System (from codebase)

### Typography
- **Font**: Figtree (Tailwind `font-sans`)
- **Headings**: `font-black italic uppercase tracking-tighter` — heavy weight, italic, all-caps, tight tracking
- **Section labels**: `text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.2em]` to `tracking-[0.4em]` with `text-slate-400 italic`
- **Numbers/Stats**: `font-black italic uppercase tracking-tighter tabular-nums` (`text-4xl` to `text-5xl`)
- **Hero titles**: `text-4xl`–`text-9xl font-black italic uppercase tracking-tighter leading-[0.85]`
- **Small meta**: `text-[8px]`–`text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.3em]`
- **Body text**: `font-bold italic` for descriptions, `text-sm`–`text-xl`

### Color Palette
- **Primary brand**: `orange-600` (main accent, CTAs, active states)
- **Primary bg**: `orange-50` (soft backgrounds, decorative blobs)
- **Dark**: `slate-900` (cards, dark sections, ranking, hero countdown)
- **Dark border**: `border-white/5`, `border-white/10` on dark sections
- **Light page bg**: `slate-50` (AdminSekolah), `white` (Cikgu, SuperAdmin)
- **Card bg**: `white` with `border-slate-100` to `border-slate-200`
- **Muted text**: `slate-400`, `slate-500`
- **Semantic colors**: `blue-600`, `red-600`, `emerald-600`, `violet-600`, `amber-600`, `rose-600`, `indigo-600`, `purple-600`
- **Ranking medals**: `yellow-400` (1st), `slate-300` (2nd), `orange-400` (3rd), `white/10` (others)
- **Selection highlight**: `selection:bg-orange-600 selection:text-white`
- **Warning**: `amber-50`/`amber-400`/`amber-800` backgrounds

### Card & Container Styles
- **Brutalist shadow**: `shadow-[8px_8px_0px_0px_rgba(15,23,42,1)]` (slate-900 offset) — used on cards and buttons (Cikgu, SuperAdmin)
- **Soft shadow**: `shadow-sm` with hover `shadow-xl` (AdminSekolah cards)
- **3D card effect**: `border-b-[8px] border-b-slate-200` with `active:translate-y-1 active:border-b-[2px]` (AdminSekolah menu)
- **Corner rounding**: `rounded-2xl` (1rem) for standard, `rounded-[2rem]`–`rounded-[3rem]` for hero/ranking/dark sections
- **Icon containers**: `w-14 h-14` to `w-20 h-20` with `rounded-[1.75rem]` for menu, `w-12 h-12` to `w-16 h-16` with `rounded-2xl` for stats
- **Borders**: `border-2` or `border-4 border-slate-900` for bold separation

### Dark Theme Elements (Ranking/CountdownHero)
- `bg-slate-900` base, white/`text-slate-400` text
- Decorative watermark: `font-black text-[8rem]–[15vw] text-white/5–10 rotate-[-10deg]`
- Glass layer: `backdrop-blur-md bg-slate-800/60 border border-white/10`
- Gradient top bar: `bg-gradient-to-r from-orange-600 via-orange-400 to-orange-600` (h-2)
- Blur accents: `bg-orange-600/10 rounded-full blur-3xl`
- Pill badge: `bg-orange-600 text-white text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.5em] rounded-full shadow-lg shadow-orange-600/20`

### Interactive States
- **Bottom-heavy border press**: `active:translate-y-1 active:border-b-[2px] transition-all duration-200`
- **Scale press**: `active:scale-95` (buttons), `active:scale-[0.98]` (list items)
- **Shadow press**: `active:translate-y-0.5 active:shadow-none` (brutalist buttons)
- **Hover lift**: `hover:-translate-y-1 transition-transform` (cards)
- **Icon hover**: `group-hover:scale-110 group-hover:rotate-3 transition-all duration-500`
- **Decorative circle**: absolute positioned, `group-hover:scale-150 transition-transform duration-700`
- **All transitions**: `transition-all duration-200` (or `duration-300`, `duration-500`, `duration-700`)

### Section Structure
- **Header pattern**: Small label + horizontal rule — `<h3 className="text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.4em] text-slate-400 italic">Section</h3>` + `<div className="h-px bg-slate-200 flex-1" />`
- **Page vertical spacing**: `space-y-12`
- **Page bottom padding**: `pb-12` (AdminSekolah), `pb-20` (ranking section)

### Grid Layouts
- **Page max-width**: `max-w-7xl` with `px-4 md:px-8`
- **Menu grid**: `grid grid-cols-2 md:grid-cols-3 lg:grid-cols-4 gap-6 md:gap-8`
- **Stats grid**: `grid grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3 gap-4 md:gap-6`
- **Two-col actions**: `grid grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 gap-8`

### Dashboard-Specific Components

#### CountdownHero
- Dark hero with live countdown timers
- Dual countdown blocks: "HARI KEJOHANAN" (meet date) and "PENYALURAN DATA" (closing date)
- Each block: label ribbon (`rotate-[-2deg]`), Countdown component, date display, live indicator pulsing dot
- Structure: decorative background text "SPIRIT" → dark container → gradient top bar → pill badge → title → countdown grid

#### Menu Grid (AdminSekolah only)
- Grouped into "Pendaftaran" and "Pengurusan" sections
- Each item: icon container (`bg-{color}-50 text-{color}-600 shadow-inner`) + label
- 3D card effect with bottom-heavy border

#### Stat Cards
- **AdminSekolah**: white card, decorative circle blob (top-right, `opacity-50`), clean layout
- **Cikgu**: brutalist (`shadow-[8px_8px_0px_0px_rgba(15,23,42,1)]`, `border-4`), solid colored icon bg
- **SuperAdmin**: brutalist shadow, icon container with inner border + shadow offset

#### Ranking Section
- Full dark (`bg-slate-900`) container with `rounded-[2.5rem]–[3rem]`
- Header with orange accent bar (`w-1.5 h-8 bg-orange-600 rounded-full`)
- List items: position number (medal-colored), house name, athlete count, points
- Empty state: dashed border `border-white/5 border-dashed`, centered text

### Mobile Navigation (AdminSekolah)
- Fixed bottom nav bar: `h-20 bg-white border-t border-slate-200 shadow-[0_-4px_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.1)]`
- 4 tabs: Home, Meets, Launch (bolt icon, orange active), Menu
- Drawer overlay: `bg-slate-900/60 backdrop-blur-sm` with rounded top sheet `rounded-t-[2.5rem]`
- Active tab: `text-orange-600` with `bg-orange-50` icon container

### Navigation Bar (Cikgu)
- `bg-white/95 backdrop-blur-md border-b-2 border-slate-100 h-20`
- "CikguPanel" branding with orange accent on "Panel"
- User button: brutalist style `border-2 border-slate-900 shadow-[4px_4px_0px_0px_rgba(15,23,42,1)]`
- Mobile hamburger: `bg-slate-900 text-orange-500` (inverted)
- Bottom border `border-b-8 border-slate-900` on mobile trigger

### Animations
- `animate-pulse` on live indicators (dots, status badges)
- `animate-in fade-in duration-300`, `slide-in-from-bottom duration-300` for mobile drawer
- `hover:scale-[1.01] duration-500` on CountdownHero
- `transform hover:-translate-y-1 transition-transform` on cards

### Empty & Error States
- Warning banners: `bg-amber-50 border-4 border-amber-400 rounded-[2rem]` with icon, title, message
- Flash errors: `border-4 border-red-400 bg-red-50 p-6 rounded-[2rem]`
- Empty ranking: dashed border, centered italic text
- Unassigned teacher: amber warning card with instruction to contact admin
