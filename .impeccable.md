## Design Context

### Users
**Cikgu (Teachers)**. The primary context is **mobile usage on the field** during Sports Day. The interface must be highly legible in bright sunlight, have large tap targets for quick data entry, and feel extremely responsive/real-time.

### Brand Personality
**Modern, Sports Vibe, Burning Spirit**. The interface should evoke energy, excitement, and professional efficiency. It's about "semangat membara" (burning spirit) - reflecting the heat and passion of a sports competition.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Bold & Energetic**. High contrast, vibrant accent colors (Orange/Red/Blue), strong typography (Figtree), and dynamic layouts. It should feel like a premium sports app (e.g., Nike, ESPN, Strava) rather than a boring administrative tool.

### Design Principles
1. **Mobile-First Utility**: Optimized for one-handed use on the field. Essential actions (entering scores, checking schedules) must be front and center.
2. **High-Visibility Contrast**: Clear separation of elements and high-contrast text to ensure usability in outdoor conditions.
3. **Real-Time Feedback**: Use subtle animations and "live" indicators to show that data is being synced and rankings are updating instantly.
4. **Vibrant Accents**: Use "burning" colors (Orange/Red) to signify energy and action, while keeping the background clean (Light Mode) for readability.
5. **Bold Typography**: Large, clear headings that establish a strong hierarchy and convey the "spirit" of the event.
6. **Card Floating & Spacing**: Ensure all dashboard elements have sufficient side padding (`px-4` on mobile) so cards do not touch the screen edges, giving a clean, "floating" appearance.

## Dashboard Design System (from codebase)

### Typography
- **Font**: Figtree (Tailwind `font-sans`)
- **Headings**: `font-black italic uppercase tracking-tighter text-slate-900`
- **Sub-headings/Context**: `text-slate-500 font-bold uppercase tracking-widest text-xs`
- **Section labels**: `text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.2em]` to `tracking-[0.4em]` with `text-slate-400 italic`
- **Numbers/Stats**: `font-black italic text-slate-900 tabular-nums` (`text-xl` to `text-3xl`)
- **Small meta**: `text-[8px]`–`text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.3em]`
- **Body text**: `font-bold italic` for descriptions, `text-sm`–`text-xl`

### Color Palette
- **Primary brand**: `orange-600` (main accent, CTAs, active states)
- **Primary bg**: `orange-50` (soft backgrounds, decorative blobs)
- **Dark**: `slate-900` (cards, dark sections, ranking)
- **Dark border**: `border-white/5`, `border-white/10` on dark sections
- **Light page bg**: `slate-50` (AdminSekolah), `white` (Cikgu, SuperAdmin)
- **Card border**: `border-slate-200`
- **Muted text**: `slate-400`, `slate-500`
- **Semantic colors**: `blue-600` (Students), `orange-600` (Events), `red-600` (Critical/Danger)
- **Selection highlight**: `selection:bg-orange-600 selection:text-white`
- **Warning**: `amber-50`/`amber-400`/`amber-800` backgrounds

### Card & Container Styles (Cikgu Dashboard Redesign)
- **Action Cards (Horizontal List)**: 
  - `bg-white border-2 border-slate-200 px-5 py-4 rounded-2xl`
  - Hover: `hover:border-{color}-600 hover:shadow-md transition-all`
  - Layout: `flex items-center gap-4`
  - Icon: `w-10 h-10 rounded-xl bg-{color}-50 text-{color}-600`
- **Stat Cards (Compact Grid)**:
  - `grid grid-cols-2 gap-3`
  - Style: `px-5 py-4 rounded-2xl`
  - Dark variant: `bg-slate-900 border-b-4 border-orange-600 text-white`
  - Light variant: `bg-white border-2 border-slate-200 text-slate-900`
- **House Position Badge**:
  - `bg-slate-900 text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-2xl flex items-center gap-4 shadow-xl border-b-4 border-orange-600`
  - Rank number: `w-10 h-10 bg-orange-600 rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-black italic text-lg`

### Interactive States
- **Hover lift**: `hover:-translate-y-0.5 transition-transform` (cards)
- **Transition**: `transition-all duration-200`

### Grid Layouts
- **Mobile Side Padding**: **CRITICAL** `px-4` horizontal padding on the main content container to prevent cards from touching screen edges.
- **Action List**: `flex flex-col gap-3`
- **Stats Grid**: `grid grid-cols-2 gap-3`
- **Table Container**: `bg-white border-2 border-slate-900 rounded-2xl overflow-hidden shadow-[6px_6px_0px_0px_rgba(15,23,42,1)]`

### Empty & Error States
- Flash errors/success: `border-2 p-3 rounded-xl font-bold text-xs` (compact style)
- Warning banners: `bg-amber-50 border-4 border-amber-400 p-6 rounded-2xl`
